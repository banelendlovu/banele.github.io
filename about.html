About

My name is Banele Ndlovu. I'm an international student-athlete from Zimbabwe at Waldorf University, studying Business in Management. My passion lies in bridging the gap that exits between Business and Technology in every community I've been given an opportunity to work, play and live in. The commercial sector has my best interests but, in the past years, my curiosity in technology has driven me to learn how to merge both industries to benefit my people. My experiance has exposed me to possibilities that have bred and improved my skillset both in business and technically. I look forward to learn more and contribute into ideas that bring a positive change, especially for my people back in Zimbabwe.
